This site provides a search tool for retrieving and displaying drug interactions between prescription medications, over-the-counter (OTC) medications, and/or herbal supplements.

This site does not create, modify, or store any information on drugs or medications - it simply retrieves and displays data made available using resources provided by the U.S. National Library of Medicine (NLM) and the National Institutes of Health (NIH). From their website:

"This product uses publicly available data from the U.S. National Library of Medicine (NLM), National Institutes of Health, Department of Health and Human Services; NLM is not responsible for the product and does not endorse or recommend this or any other product."

https://rxnav.nlm.nih.gov/APIsOverview.html

With that in mind, feel free to use this website to search for information on possible interactions between prescription medications, over-the-counter (OTC) medications, and/or herbal supplement that you may find interesting or want to learn more about.
